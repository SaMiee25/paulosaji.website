About Us

Who We Are?

PAUL OSAJI & CO is a leading real estate consultancy firm, providing exceptional services since 1997. With our headquarters in Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, and additional offices in Ikeja, Asaba, and Abuja, we offer comprehensive solutions nationwide. Registered with the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (CAP E 13 LFN 2007).

Right from inception, we have continously focused on building a world-class Real Estate consulting practice and we are not slowing down ever.
OUR MISSION

“To provide premium and innovative real estate consultancy services with the aim of giving our very best at all times to the benefit of all stakeholders and to be the industry First rate in Nigeria and beyond”

OUR VISION

“To build a team that consistently challenges itself to deliver limitless possibilities to our clients and to give them maximum value for their investment”

CORE VALUES

> Seek to establish a culture that will foster dynamism, high ethical standards, professionalism and teamwork.
> Making performance a daily commitment.
> Achieve differentiation from competitors by developing an uncompromising committment to superior quality in all aspects of our services.
> Seek to understand and show responsiveness to the needs of our clients and ensure their satisfaction

Our Partners

Mr. Paul Osaji is the founding partner and holds a Bachelor of Technology Degree in Estate Management from Rivers State University of Science and Technology(1984). Paul is a fellow of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers. He is duly registered by the Estate Surveyors & Valuers. Registration Board OF Nigeria (ESVARBON) and an Accredited Senior Appraiser of the American Society of Appraisers.He is a member of the International Real Estate Federation (FIABCI) and Institute of Directors (IoD). He was the former Commissioner for Works, Delta State and the Special Project Director for the construction of Asaba Airport, Delta State.

Chief Ofili Kevin is the Senior Partner and holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Estate Management from Enugu State University of Science and Technology(1997). He also holds a post graduate Diploma in Financial Management from the University of Ibadan (1999) and a Master's degree in Project Management from University of Lagos (2006). He is a fellow of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers. He is duly registered by the Estate Surveyors & Valuers Registration Board OF Nigeria (ESVARBON) and an Accredited Senior Appraiser of the American Society of Appraisers. Kevin is a member of Nigeria Institute of Management (NIM), African Real Estate Society and Nigeria Institute of Arbitrators

Mrs. Angela Ikade is the Managing Partner of the firm. She holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Estate Management from Obafemi Awolowo University Ile Ife (1989). She is an Associate member of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers. She is duly registered by the Estate Surveyors & Valuers Registration Board OF Nigeria (ESVARBON) and an Accredited Senior Appraiser of the American Society of Appraisers. She joined Paul Osaji & Co. as the Head of Valuation Department and was later appointed as Group Head, Valuation, Advanced Valuation and Development a position she still occupies as the Managing Partner.
